# <Star Wars - Andor> Storyline

Beginning five years before the events of Rogue One (2016) and A New Hope (1977), 
the series follows an ensemble cast of characters during the time that a Rebel Alliance is forming in opposition to the Galactic Empire. 
One of these characters is Cassian Andor, a thief who becomes a revolutionary and eventually joins the Rebellion.
